Vonk Consulting is a one-man hardware technology consulting firm that can help you to:

  • develop a new sensor or actuator for your application, or identify an off the shelf option
  • establish and validate electromechanical models
  • generate a transducer design that is robust across process tolerances
  • create a new fabrication or assembly process
  • fix a burning problem in your factory or fab
  • develop custom software tools for your team's long-term efficiency

The skills that enable these outcomes include:

  • developing predictive lumped or FEA models for new problems
  • analytical and numerical design optimization
  • Monte Carlo analysis based upon upstream process limits
  • analog circuit design for operation and DFT
  • MEMS process development
  • strong software development experience

DR. JOSEPH C. DOLL

I started Vonk Consulting in 2018, after leading hardware engineering teams at SiTime and Apple.

At SiTime, I led the MEMS design team in developing quartz crystal alternatives. The custom simulation tools, circuits, and resonator designs that we developed contributed to industry-leading timing performance and a successful acquisition.

At Apple, I managed the 3D Touch electrical engineering architecture team for the iPhone X, served as technical lead on several other projects, and consulted with teams throughout the company on assorted problems.

I was educated at UC Berkeley and Stanford. At Stanford, I developed MEMS-based tools to study the sense of hearing at the molecular scale. My publication record includes 40+ journal and conference publications, 6+ patents, and one book.
